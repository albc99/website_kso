For any inquiries regarding student life at MSU, information requests, or job postings (which we feature on our Instagram page and through internal advertisements), feel free to reach out to us 24/7 through the contact methods listed above. We strive to respond to all queries within 1-2 days, though please allow up to 7 business days for a reply. For confidential or urgent matters, you are welcome to directly contact the President or Vice President of our organization (details available on the About Us page). Please note that our organization operates according to the university calendar.
